Privacy Policy

SoundBrief

Last Updated: March 2026

SoundBrief respects your privacy. This application is designed to operate without collecting or storing personal information from its users.

⸻

Information Collection

SoundBrief does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information from users.

The app does not require users to create accounts or provide personal details such as:

• Name
• Email address
• Phone number
• Location information
• Contacts
• Photos or media
• Payment information

SoundBrief does not track users or maintain user profiles.

⸻

Audio Content

SoundBrief processes publicly available podcast content to provide playback and AI-generated summaries.

The application does not record audio from your device and does not access your microphone.

⸻

Local Device Data

Some temporary data may be stored locally on the device to allow the app to function properly (for example, caching podcast content or summaries).

This information remains on the user’s device and is not transmitted to SoundBrief servers.

⸻

Third-Party Services

SoundBrief may rely on external services to retrieve publicly available podcast content or generate summaries. These services process content only for functionality and do not receive personal user data.

⸻

Advertising

SoundBrief may display advertisements to support the free version of the app. Advertising providers may use device identifiers according to their own privacy policies.

Users can manage ad tracking preferences in their device privacy settings.

⸻

Children’s Privacy

SoundBrief does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

⸻

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect improvements to the app. Updates will be reflected by the “Last Updated” date above.
