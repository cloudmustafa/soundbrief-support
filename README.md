SoundBrief – Support

Welcome to the SoundBrief support page. SoundBrief helps you stay informed by turning podcasts and audio content into easy-to-read summaries using AI. This page provides guidance on how to use the app and answers common questions.

⸻

Getting Started

SoundBrief allows you to follow podcasts and audio sources, listen to episodes, and read AI-generated summaries to quickly understand the most important insights.

The app is organized into four main sections:

• Listen
• Read
• Channels
• Settings

⸻

Features

Channels

The Channels tab allows you to discover and follow podcasts and audio sources.

You can:
• Browse suggested podcasts
• Search for podcasts by keyword
• Add podcasts to your feed
• Remove podcasts at any time

Tap the + icon next to a podcast to follow it.

⸻

Listen

The Listen tab allows you to play podcast episodes directly inside the app.

Features include:

• Play / Pause audio
• Stop playback
• Adjust playback speed
• View episode details
• Access AI-powered tools

⸻

Read (AI Summaries)

The Read tab generates a short summary of podcast episodes so you can quickly understand the main ideas.

Summaries include:

• Key highlights from the episode
• Main discussion topics
• Important insights from the conversation

This feature is designed for users who want to quickly absorb information without listening to the entire episode.

⸻

Deep Dive

The Deep Dive feature provides a more detailed analysis of podcast discussions.

It may include:

• Expanded explanation of topics
• Context around discussions
• Additional insights extracted from the episode

⸻

Compare

The Compare feature allows users to compare perspectives from different podcast sources on similar topics.

This helps users better understand differing viewpoints and build a more balanced perspective.

⸻

Daily Mix

SoundBrief automatically creates a Daily Mix based on the podcasts you follow.

This provides a quick way to listen to new episodes and explore recommended content.

⸻

Ads

SoundBrief may display advertisements to support the free version of the app. These ads do not affect podcast content or summaries.
